The goal for this project is to explore the evolution and interactions of “fish”. It has also been gamified, so that the user can better understand the experience of these organisms. 

The “fish” act more like single cellular organisms at times. For the most part, they divide in half once they reach their maximum reproduction size. One half experiences mutations of traits like field of vision, speed, decisiveness, etc… Fish grow and gain cells by eating food or smaller fish. They also lose cells due to exhaustion from swimming. Hunting and fleeing is based on size and proximity.
